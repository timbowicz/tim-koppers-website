PSV '100 year'

20,000 people in the PSV stadium in Eindhoven installed our app to make the 100th anniversary of the club a show to never forget. Watch the result.

Be part of the show

To enhance the effects, you entered your section of the stadium in the app. When the flashlight part began, each section of the stadium flashed in its own rhythm.

3... 2... 1... GO

All phones were synced to a server clock, so the show started at exactly 11 pm.
