Mitsubishi Control 10

2008

An interactive game website to launch the 10th version of the Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution sports car. The ‘Evo X’ accelerates from 0–100 km/h in 4.8 seconds — are you capable enough to handle such power? Created together with Lukkien.

Try for yourself

Finish all ten games on the website and you could win a test drive in this fast car.

FWA award

The site won the prestigious FWA (Favourite Website Award) — the digital equivalent of an Oscar.
