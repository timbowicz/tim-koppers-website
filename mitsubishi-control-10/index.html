Mitsubishi Control 10

2008

A game website for the launch of the tenth Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution. The Evo X goes from 0 to 100 km/h in 4.8 seconds. Can you handle that power? Made with Lukkien.

Try for yourself

Finish all ten games on the site and you could win a test drive in the real car.

FWA award

The site won an FWA, the Favourite Website Award: the web design equivalent of an Oscar.
