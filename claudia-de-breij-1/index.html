Claudia de Breij

The theater show ‘iClaudia’ is about how busy we are meeting each other in digital space — an alter ego in Second Life, the hottest dates online, keeping up your social profiles… but what about the real world?

Claudia Avatar

The show is about being in the real world for just once, before we move to the digital world forever. For the poster we created a half human / half avatar Claudia.
