Madame Tussauds | interactive installation

Madame Tussauds in Amsterdam faced a nice challenge: how to entertain visitors in a digital and interactive way? Together with brainstorm & concept we created an interactive installation where visitors star in a movie with the famous Charlie Chaplin.

Do the famous walk

Stand on the marker to activate the movie. You see yourself on a big TV screen while Charlie Chaplin appears next to you — and challenges you to do his famous walk.

Social sharing

Every scene is recorded and can be shared by e-mail or on Facebook afterwards.

On average around 900 videos are shared every week, and every shared video is watched about three times.

1 million videos recorded!

The counter passed 1,051,143 recorded videos in early 2019 — and around 25% of those videos are shared.
