Luchtverkeersleiding | recruitment game

2013

How do you reach young, potential future air traffic controllers in the digital era? Right: by making a challenging game that puts their air traffic control skills to the test.

Top 10 in the App Store

We developed the game for three platforms: iOS, Android and Facebook.com. In the first week after release the game reached a top 10 position in the Dutch App Store.

100,000 downloads in a month

Within a month the game was downloaded over 100,000 times. The 30 best players were invited to battle each other at the Dutch Championship ‘Take Control of the Tower’.

1 million downloads

In 2017 we passed one million combined downloads on iOS and Android worldwide. The average session time is around six minutes — remarkably high for this kind of game.

National event

The 30 best players were invited to the headquarters of Luchtverkeersleiding Nederland, where they battled for the national championship: 30 iPads lined up and a scoreboard with realtime scores.
