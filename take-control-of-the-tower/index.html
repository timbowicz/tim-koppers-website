Luchtverkeersleiding
recruitment game

2013

How do you get young people interested in a career in air traffic control? Give them a game that puts their skills to the test.

Top 10 in the App Store

We built the game for iOS, Android and Facebook. Within a week of release it hit the top 10 of the Dutch App Store.

100,000 downloads in a month

Over 100,000 downloads in the first month. The 30 best players were invited to the Dutch Championship ‘Take Control of the Tower’.

1 million downloads

In 2017 the game passed one million downloads worldwide. Average session time: around six minutes, remarkably long for this kind of game.

National event

The final took place at the headquarters of Luchtverkeersleiding Nederland: 30 iPads in a row and a live scoreboard.
