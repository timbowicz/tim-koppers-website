Medu.game
game-based learning platform

2021 — present

Medu.game is my own product: a game-based learning platform for healthcare professionals that closes the gap between theory and practice. Play realistic clinical scenarios in 3D, make decisions under pressure and get instant feedback — in the browser or in the app.

More than 2,000 professionals have trained with Medu, from nursing students to anesthesiologists. Discover more at medu.game.

Learn by doing

From resuscitation with an AED to the ABCDE protocol: you don’t read about it, you do it. Safe to fail, instant feedback after every decision, and scenarios you can repeat as often as you need.

Realistic 3D scenarios

Immersive hospital environments — an emergency department, an operating room, a patient’s home — with AI-driven patient conversations, voice and a story that reacts to your choices.

Any device, anywhere

Runs right in the browser and as an app on iOS and Android, with synced progress. Schools and hospitals plug it into their own learning environment (LTI).

Build your own scenarios

A visual builder lets educators assemble new cases from building blocks — no code required.
