Extrema Outdoor XO

2013

How do you involve a party-ready crowd in the opening show of a dance festival? That was the question from Extrema, the festival’s organizers. Our answer: “why not make an interactive show you can be part of…”

Be part of the show

Every smartphone can play sounds, flash its light, vibrate and display colors. That is exactly what we used — in choreographed patterns, driven by the music and the video on the big screens.

Be in sync

When you installed the app, its countdown clock synced to a web server — the same countdown ran on the big screens. Everybody got the same experience, and no internet connection was required during the show.

Feel the rhythm

Flashlights blinking on the beat of the music.
