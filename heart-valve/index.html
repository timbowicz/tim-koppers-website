Heart Valve
serious learning game

2018

This serious learning game was created for Edwards Lifesciences. It is aimed at general practitioners and other medical professionals, helping them improve their knowledge and skills in cardiac examination and auscultation.

The Heart Valve game is accredited for one official accreditation point: players who log in with their BIG registration number and achieve a good score automatically receive the point — a first for a serious learning game.

Play different patient cases

In the Heart Valve game you work like a medical detective. In your virtual practice you receive patients for a consult: you engage in a dialogue, decide which questions are relevant and carry out physical examinations — including auscultation of the heart, with real murmur sounds the player learns to assess.

Developed with cardiologists

The game was developed in collaboration with cardiologists Dr. Renée van den Brink (AMC) and Dr. Janneke Wittekoek (Heartlife klinieken), who provided the patient cases and medical background.

Listen to the heart

Perform different physical examinations and listen to the heart to hear exactly what the murmur sounds like.
